Two Planners, One Standard Of Craft

Yaadgar Events Planner was founded on a simple idea: every celebration, big or small, deserves the same level of care. Led by Abdullah Sheikh and Huzaifa Wazir, our team plans and executes corporate functions, birthdays and private parties from first consultation to final tear-down.
